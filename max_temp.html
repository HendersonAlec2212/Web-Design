    Latitude vs. Max Temperature

    This plot displays the change in temperature as one approaches the equator or zero on the graph.


    It is easy to spot this trend and for good reason; the equator has a higher amount of direct sunlight when compared to the rest of the earth, lending to higher overall temperatures.
